Salah Guide

Learn the Five Daily Salah

The Five Daily Salah

Salah is the second pillar of Islam and one of the most important acts of worship. Muslims perform five obligatory Salah throughout the day to maintain a connection with Allah.

"وَأَقِيمُوا الصَّلَاةَ وَآتُوا الزَّكَاةَ"
"And establish prayer and give charity" - Quran 2:43

Salah Times

1

Fajr (Dawn Salah)

From dawn until sunrise - 2 Rakats
2

Dhuhr (Noon Salah)

From midday until afternoon - 4 Rakats
3

Asr (Afternoon Salah)

From afternoon until sunset - 4 Rakats
4

Maghrib (Sunset Salah)

From sunset until twilight - 3 Rakats
5

Isha (Night Salah)

From twilight until dawn - 4 Rakats

Wudu (Ablution) Guide

Wudu is the ritual washing required before Salah. It purifies the body and prepares the mind for worship.

1

Intention (Niyyah)

Make the intention in your heart to perform wudu for Salah

2

Wash Hands

Wash both hands up to the wrists, starting with the right hand

3

Rinse Mouth

Take water in your mouth and rinse thoroughly, then spit it out

4

Sniff Water

Take water in your nose and sniff it, then blow it out

5

Wash Face

Wash your entire face from forehead to chin and ear to ear

6

Wash Arms

Wash both arms up to the elbows, starting with the right arm

7

Wipe Head

Wipe your head with wet hands from front to back

8

Wash Feet

Wash both feet up to the ankles, starting with the right foot

After Wudu Supplication

أَشْهَدُ أَنْ لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا اللَّهُ وَحْدَهُ لَا شَرِيكَ لَهُ وَأَشْهَدُ أَنَّ مُحَمَّدًا عَبْدُهُ وَرَسُولُهُ
Ashhadu an la ilaha illa Allah wahdahu la sharika lah, wa ashhadu anna Muhammadan 'abduhu wa rasuluh
I bear witness that there is no deity except Allah alone, without partner, and I bear witness that Muhammad is His servant and messenger

Complete Salah Guide

Follow these detailed steps to perform each Salah correctly. Each step includes the Arabic text, transliteration, and translation.

Salah Structure Overview

1 Takbir
2 Qiyam (Standing)
3 Ruku (Bowing)
4 Sujood (Prostration)
5 Tashahhud
6 Salam

1. Takbir (Opening)

🙏
Raise hands to ears level
اللهُ أَكْبَر
Allahu Akbar
Allah is the Greatest

Raise both hands to ears level and say "Allahu Akbar" while standing. This begins the Salah.

2. Qiyam (Standing Position)

🧍
Stand straight, hands on chest
الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ رَبِّ الْعَالَمِينَ * الرَّحْمَنِ الرَّحِيمِ * مَالِكِ يَوْمِ الدِّينِ
Alhamdu lillahi rabbil 'alameen * Ar-rahman ir-rahim * Maliki yawmi ad-din
[All] praise is [due] to Allah, Lord of the worlds * The Entirely Merciful, the Especially Merciful * Sovereign of the Day of Recompense

Recite Surah Al-Fatiha in the first two rakats of every Salah. Stand straight with hands folded on chest.

3. Ruku (Bowing)

🙇
Bend at waist, hands on knees
سُبْحَانَ رَبِّيَ الْعَظِيم
Subhana rabbiyal 'azeem
Glory to my Lord, the Great

Bend at waist, place hands on knees, and recite this 3 times. Keep back straight and parallel to ground.

4. Standing from Ruku

🧍
Return to standing position
سَمِعَ اللَّهُ لِمَنْ حَمِدَهُ
Sami' Allahu liman hamidah
Allah hears those who praise Him

Return to standing position while saying this, then say "Rabbana wa lakal hamd" (Our Lord, to You is praise).

5. Sujood (Prostration)

🤲
Forehead, nose, hands, knees, toes on ground
سُبْحَانَ رَبِّيَ الأَعْلَى
Subhana rabbiyal a'la
Glory to my Lord, the Most High

Place forehead, nose, hands, knees, and toes on ground. Recite this 3 times. This is the most humble position in Salah.

6. Sitting Between Sujood

🧘
Sit briefly between prostrations
رَبِّ اغْفِرْ لِي
Rabbighfir li
My Lord, forgive me

Sit briefly between the two prostrations and say this supplication.

7. Tashahhud (Final Sitting)

🧘
Sit with right index finger raised
التَّحِيَّاتُ لِلَّهِ وَالصَّلَوَاتُ وَالطَّيِّبَاتُ
At-tahiyyatu lillahi wa-s-salawatu wa-t-tayyibat
All greetings, prayers, and good words are for Allah

Sit and recite this in the final rakat of each Salah. Raise your right index finger while reciting.

8. Salam (Ending)

👋
Turn head right and left
السَّلَامُ عَلَيْكُمْ وَرَحْمَةُ اللَّهِ
As-salamu 'alaykum wa rahmatullah
Peace be upon you and Allah's mercy

Turn head right and left while saying this to end the Salah. This completes your Salah.

Common Salah Questions & Troubleshooting

What if I forget a step?

If you forget a step, perform the missing action and continue. If you realize after completing the Salah, perform the prostration of forgetfulness (sajdah as-sahw).

What if I break my wudu during Salah?

If your wudu breaks during Salah, stop immediately, perform wudu again, and restart the Salah from the beginning.

Can I perform Salah in English?

While the Quran and formal Salah must be in Arabic, personal supplications (Du'a) can be made in any language you understand.

What if I can't find the Qibla direction?

Make your best effort to face the Qibla. If uncertain, pray in any direction and Allah will accept it. Use a compass or Qibla app for accuracy.

Can I perform Salah sitting down?

Yes, if you're ill or unable to stand. Sitting Salah is acceptable, and if you can't sit, you can perform Salah lying down with gestures.

What if I miss a Salah time?

Make up the missed Salah as soon as possible. It's better to perform Salah late than not at all. Don't combine Salah unless traveling or ill.

Common Salah Mistakes to Avoid

❌

Rushing Through Salah

Take your time with each step. Salah is a conversation with Allah, not a race. The Prophet said, "The worst thief is the one who steals from his Salah."

❌

Not Understanding What You're Saying

Learn the meanings of what you recite. Understanding brings more concentration and spiritual benefit to your prayer.

❌

Incorrect Posture

Ensure proper posture in each position. In ruku, keep your back straight. In sujood, place all required body parts on the ground.

❌

Looking Around During Salah

Keep your gaze down during Salah. The Prophet said, "Let not any one of you look around during Salah, for it is a form of heedlessness."

❌

Not Making Intention

Always make the intention (niyyah) in your heart before starting Salah. This is essential for the Salah to be valid.

Essential Salah Supplications

Before Salah

أَعُوذُ بِاللَّهِ مِنَ الشَّيْطَانِ الرَّجِيم
A'udhu billahi min ash-shaytan ir-rajim
I seek refuge in Allah from Satan, the accursed

Recite this before starting your Salah to seek protection from distractions.

After Salah

أَسْتَغْفِرُ اللَّهَ
Astaghfirullah
I seek forgiveness from Allah

Say this 3 times after completing your Salah to seek forgiveness.

Morning Remembrance

سُبْحَانَ اللَّهِ وَبِحَمْدِهِ سُبْحَانَ اللَّهِ الْعَظِيم
Subhanallahi wa bihamdihi, subhanallahil 'azeem
Glory to Allah and praise to Him, Glory to Allah the Great

Recite this 100 times in the morning for great rewards.

Evening Remembrance

اللَّهُمَّ أَنْتَ رَبِّي لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا أَنْتَ
Allahumma anta rabbi, la ilaha illa anta
O Allah, You are my Lord, there is no deity except You

This comprehensive supplication can be made at any time.

Seeking Guidance

رَبِّ اشْرَحْ لِي صَدْرِي وَيَسِّرْ لِي أَمْرِي
Rabbi ishrah li sadri wa yassir li amri
My Lord, expand my chest for me and make my task easy for me

Recite this when facing difficulties or important decisions.

Gratitude

الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ رَبِّ الْعَالَمِينَ
Alhamdulillahi rabbil 'alameen
Praise be to Allah, Lord of all the worlds

Express gratitude to Allah for His countless blessings.
