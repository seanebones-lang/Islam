Daily Duas by Category

Morning Duas

Start your day with these beautiful supplications

Dua for Waking Up

"الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ الَّذِي أَحْيَانَا بَعْدَ مَا أَمَاتَنَا وَإِلَيْهِ النُّشُورُ"
"Praise be to Allah who gave us life after causing us to die, and to Him is the resurrection."
Bukhari

Dua for Protection in the Morning

"أَعُوذُ بِكَلِمَاتِ اللَّهِ التَّامَّاتِ مِنْ شَرِّ مَا خَلَقَ"
"I seek refuge in the perfect words of Allah from the evil of what He has created."
Muslim

Dua for Good in This World and Next

"رَبَّنَا آتِنَا فِي الدُّنْيَا حَسَنَةً وَفِي الآخِرَةِ حَسَنَةً وَقِنَا عَذَابَ النَّارِ"
"Our Lord, give us good in this world and good in the hereafter, and protect us from the punishment of the Fire."
Quran 2:201

Dua for Forgiveness

"رَبِّ اغْفِرْ لِي وَتُبْ عَلَيَّ إِنَّكَ أَنْتَ التَّوَّابُ الرَّحِيمُ"
"My Lord, forgive me and accept my repentance, for You are the Accepter of Repentance, the Merciful."
Tirmidhi

Evening Duas

End your day with these peaceful supplications

Dua Before Sleeping

"بِاسْمِكَ اللَّهُمَّ أَمُوتُ وَأَحْيَا"
"In Your name, O Allah, I die and I live."
Bukhari

Dua for Evening Protection

"أَمْسَيْنَا وَأَمْسَى الْمُلْكُ لِلَّهِ، وَالْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ، لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا اللَّهُ"
"We have reached the evening and the dominion belongs to Allah, and praise be to Allah. There is no god but Allah."
Muslim

Dua for Forgiveness at Night

"اللَّهُمَّ أَمْسَيْنَا وَأَمْسَى الْمُلْكُ لِلَّهِ، وَالْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ، لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا اللَّهُ وَحْدَهُ لَا شَرِيكَ لَهُ"
"O Allah, we have reached the evening and the dominion belongs to Allah, and praise be to Allah. There is no god but Allah alone, without partner."
Muslim

Dua for Protection from Night

"أَعُوذُ بِكَلِمَاتِ اللَّهِ التَّامَّاتِ مِنْ شَرِّ مَا خَلَقَ"
"I seek refuge in the perfect words of Allah from the evil of what He has created."
Muslim

Prayer Duas

Duas to recite during and after prayers

Dua After Prayer

"سُبْحَانَ اللَّهِ وَالْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ وَلَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا اللَّهُ وَاللَّهُ أَكْبَرُ"
"Glory be to Allah, and praise be to Allah, and there is no god but Allah, and Allah is the Greatest."
Muslim

Dua Before Prayer

"أَعُوذُ بِاللَّهِ مِنَ الشَّيْطَانِ الرَّجِيمِ"
"I seek refuge in Allah from Satan, the accursed."
Quran 16:98

Dua in Ruku (Bowing)

"سُبْحَانَ رَبِّيَ الْعَظِيمِ"
"Glory to my Lord, the Great."
Muslim

Dua in Sujud (Prostration)

"سُبْحَانَ رَبِّيَ الْأَعْلَى"
"Glory to my Lord, the Most High."
Muslim

Dua Between Sujud

"رَبِّ اغْفِرْ لِي وَارْحَمْنِي وَاهْدِنِي وَارْزُقْنِي"
"My Lord, forgive me, have mercy on me, guide me, and provide for me."
Tirmidhi

Travel Duas

Duas for safe travels and journeys

Dua Before Traveling

"سُبْحَانَ الَّذِي سَخَّرَ لَنَا هَذَا وَمَا كُنَّا لَهُ مُقْرِنِينَ"
"Glory to Him who has subjected this to us, and we could not have done it ourselves."
Quran 43:13

Dua for Safe Return

"آيِبُونَ تَائِبُونَ عَابِدُونَ لِرَبِّنَا حَامِدُونَ"
"We return, repentant, worshipping, and praising our Lord."
Muslim

Dua for Protection During Travel

"اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنْ وَعْثَاءِ السَّفَرِ وَكَآبَةِ الْمَنْظَرِ"
"O Allah, I seek refuge in You from the hardships of travel and the grief of return."
Muslim

Dua When Entering a Vehicle

"بِسْمِ اللَّهِ مَجْرَاهَا وَمُرْسَاهَا إِنَّ رَبِّي لَغَفُورٌ رَّحِيمٌ"
"In the name of Allah is its course and its mooring. Indeed, my Lord is Forgiving and Merciful."
Quran 11:41

General Duas

Versatile duas for everyday use

Dua for Guidance

"رَبِّ اشْرَحْ لِي صَدْرِي وَيَسِّرْ لِي أَمْرِي"
"My Lord, expand for me my breast and make easy for me my affair."
Quran 20:25-26

Dua for Knowledge

"رَبِّ زِدْنِي عِلْمًا"
"My Lord, increase me in knowledge."
Quran 20:114

Dua for Patience

"رَبَّنَا أَفْرِغْ عَلَيْنَا صَبْرًا وَثَبِّتْ أَقْدَامَنَا"
"Our Lord, pour upon us patience and plant firmly our feet."
Quran 2:250

Dua for Health

"اللَّهُمَّ عَافِنِي فِي بَدَنِي، اللَّهُمَّ عَافِنِي فِي سَمْعِي، اللَّهُمَّ عَافِنِي فِي بَصَرِي"
"O Allah, grant me health in my body, O Allah, grant me health in my hearing, O Allah, grant me health in my sight."
Tirmidhi

Dua for Family

"رَبَّنَا هَبْ لَنَا مِنْ أَزْوَاجِنَا وَذُرِّيَّاتِنَا قُرَّةَ أَعْيُنٍ"
"Our Lord, grant us from among our wives and offspring comfort to our eyes."
Quran 25:74

Dua for Success

"رَبَّنَا آتِنَا مِن لَّدُنكَ رَحْمَةً وَهَيِّئْ لَنَا مِنْ أَمْرِنَا رَشَدًا"
"Our Lord, grant us from Yourself mercy and prepare for us from our affair right guidance."
Quran 18:10

Protection Duas

Duas seeking Allah's protection and safety

Dua for Protection from Evil

"أَعُوذُ بِاللَّهِ مِنَ الشَّيْطَانِ الرَّجِيمِ"
"I seek refuge in Allah from Satan, the accursed."
Quran 16:98

Dua for Protection from Harm

"حَسْبِيَ اللَّهُ لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا هُوَ عَلَيْهِ تَوَكَّلْتُ وَهُوَ رَبُّ الْعَرْشِ الْعَظِيمِ"
"Sufficient for me is Allah; there is no deity except Him. On him I have relied, and He is the Lord of the Great Throne."
Quran 9:129

Dua for Protection from Calamities

"اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنْ جَهْدِ الْبَلَاءِ وَدَرَكِ الشَّقَاءِ"
"O Allah, I seek refuge in You from the hardship of trials and the depth of misery."
Tirmidhi

Dua for Protection from Enemies

"اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنْ شَرِّ مَا خَلَقْتَ"
"O Allah, I seek refuge in You from the evil of what You have created."
Muslim

Dua for Protection from Poverty

"اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنَ الْفَقْرِ وَالْقِلَّةِ وَالذِّلَّةِ"
"O Allah, I seek refuge in You from poverty, scarcity, and humiliation."
Abu Dawud

Dua for Protection from Hellfire

"اللَّهُمَّ أَجِرْنِي مِنَ النَّارِ"
"O Allah, protect me from the Fire."
Muslim
