Community Guidelines & Moderation

Our commitment to maintaining a safe, respectful, and educational environment for all Muslims worldwide

Respectful Communication

  • Use respectful and kind language
  • Address others as "brother" or "sister"
  • Maintain Islamic etiquette (adab)
  • Listen with an open heart
  • Disagree respectfully

Islamic Content

  • Share authentic Islamic knowledge
  • Provide sources for claims
  • Respect different schools of thought
  • Focus on learning and growth
  • Seek knowledge with humility

Community Spirit

  • Support fellow Muslims
  • Help new Muslims learn
  • Share beneficial resources
  • Encourage positive discussions
  • Build lasting friendships

Prohibited Content

  • No inappropriate language
  • No personal attacks
  • No spam or advertisements
  • No extremist content
  • No anti-Islamic material

Auto-Moderation System

Our advanced AI-powered moderation system automatically protects the community from inappropriate content while maintaining a welcoming environment for authentic Islamic discussions.

Content Filtering

Automatically detects and filters inappropriate language, spam, and harmful content

Islamic Validation

Ensures respectful use of Islamic terms and maintains Islamic etiquette

Spam Protection

Prevents spam, advertisements, and malicious content from reaching the community

Personal Safety

Protects against personal attacks, harassment, and inappropriate behavior

Warning System

Our progressive warning system helps guide community members toward better behavior while maintaining a supportive environment.

Level 1: Gentle Reminder

Content is flagged for review. User receives a gentle reminder about community guidelines.

Level 2: Formal Warning

Repeated violations result in a formal warning and temporary posting restrictions.

Level 3: Account Review

Serious violations lead to account review and potential temporary suspension.

Appeal Process
